About Us

Thakur College of Engineering & Technology is pleased to announce the 2nd edition of Hackanova, our most awaited technical event. TCET's Hackanova aims at holding hackathons on the social themes and building their solutions through technology, spreading an endless technical exposure. TCET's Hackanova gathers students from institutions all around India and gives them the opportunity to flaunt their technical skills.
